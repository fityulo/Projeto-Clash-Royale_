Simple ways to get cards


Clash Royale is a game based on collecting cards
and using them to form a versatile and effective deck
so it is essential that we have the maximum diversity of cards
to create useful decks for your evolution in the game.
Here we will show you the ways simpler to obtain cards within the game.



Chests:


Chests are the most efficient way to obtain
different types of cards, from common to champions.
Its versatility is due to its easy obtainment and can be obtained
by winning matches tournaments and even by accumulating crowns
in addition to being able to be obtained for a fee.
Its quantity and abundance completely depends on the arena
in which you are present, the more advanced it is, the more abundant it is.


Store:


The Store is a very useful place to obtain cards
having a small but efficient in terms of quantity.
thus being a massive source of some types of letters.
Cards of legendary rarity can even be found from arena 10 onwards.



Exchanges:


Exchanges are a recently introduced method in clans
to generate contact and development of new players.
In which we can use exchange tokens for
common, rare, epic and legendary cards respectively.
Such tokens can be obtained through events, tournaments, among other ways.




Donate and request letters:


Donating and requesting cards are actions found in clans
where you can request and donate cards among
your members in exchange for xp and other resources such as gold.
