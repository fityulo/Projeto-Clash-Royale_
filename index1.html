What is Clash Royale?


Clash Royale is a real-time strategy game for mobile devices developed by Supercell.
It is a card game where players can use troop,spell, and trap cards to fight other players
in battles involving two or four players.

More for Clash Royale:


In this game, players start with eight cards
in their deck and have to use these cards to win the game.
Players use these cards to attack enemies and destroy enemy towers to win matches.
Players can also use cards to defend their own tower.
Clash Royale is a fun and challenging game that requires a lot of strategy and skill.
If you like strategy games, then you will definitely like Clash Royale.


Clans:


Clash Royale also has a clan system, there are groups that people join to chat and play together.
Each person can create their own clan, at a cost of 1000 game coins, and you can customize it to your liking
including your clan shield, your motto, your requirements and your region.





Game basics:


Clash Royale is a strategy game in which you will fight against a random opponent.
The objective is to destroy enemy towers using cards that can be unlocked over time.
Some of them are brought from another Supercell game: Clash of Clans.
Each card during a match has an elixir price
a resource used during matches, accumulated over time to serve
as energy to position the cards selected in the menu on the battlefield;
There is also the option of playing against a friend, playing some modes
having an ally to help annihilate the opponent's towers.
