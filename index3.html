Easily obtainable resources


Resources such as

gold and
gems

are extremely necessary for various actions:
such as evolution to strengthen cards, as well as to obtain experience
and star points that gradually evolve the level of the king's tower.
Such resources can be obtained in several different ways, on this page we will
show you the simplest ways to obtain such resources, whether paid or free.





Gold:


Gold is an extremely important resource used for the most basic situations
such as evolving cards, creating clans, exchanging cards (when they are maximized).
Therefore, we will show you all the ways to obtain it.


Gems:


Gems are the most complicated items to obtain initially
as they have the ability to replace and buy most things in the game
making them a very valuable type of item.
Here we will show you how to obtain them in a variety of ways.


Chests:


Chests are the main sources of wealth regardless of the topic in which we obtain
relative amounts of gold and gems depending on the arena you are in.

Chests take a certain amount of time to unlock, and this time varies depending on rarity.
Chests obtained in other ways can be opened immediately.



The time needed to open chests is:

Wooden chest (tutorial only) : 5 seconds
Silver chest, Crate of gold : 3 hours
Golden chest, Crate full of gold : 8 hours
Magic chest, Giant chest, Epic Chest, Crate overflowing with gold :12 hours
Wild Chest, Legendary chest, Lightning Mega Chest,Champion Chest :1 day

The following chests can be opened without a wait time:


Lucky chest, King's Chest/Legendary King's Chest, New level chest





Challenges:


Challenges are an invaluable source of wealth as they have several prizes
like lots of gold, gems and even valuable chests.
There are several types of challenges, the most common of which are the
classic challenge, strategic challenge and the grand challenge, each with a different prize.



Challenges may appear below the main event between limited-time events:


These special challenges contain different game modes such as
elixir capture, double or triple elixir, among others.




Offers:


Offerings are the easiest ways to gather treasure.
In addition to being very efficient, most of them are paid.
They usually have large quantities of gold and gems.




Domain:


Domains are the most complicated ways to obtain resources
because to obtain such rewards we have to use the same cards several times
and complete different achievements
to obtain the aforementioned treasures, including cards, jokers, gold and gems.
